Project One

'Clamps' was a freshman project. We were required to build a robot to complete a task of our choice. My teammate and I decided to work on a 'pick-up' robot. Its original purpose was to pick up toys around a toddler's playroom, but we realized its purpose could further be expanded as a garbage collecting robot. The image above is a working prototype of the robot. We intended to 3D print a box matching the dimensions of the one above using the Solidworks software, but decided to keep the more conservative option of a cardboard box.
