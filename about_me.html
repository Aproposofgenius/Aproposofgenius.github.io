About Me

My name is Omomhene Chloe Eimunjeze. I graduated from Vaughn College of Aeronautics and Technology in Queens, New York with a Bachelors in Mechatronics Engineering, a major which unites the principles of mechanics, electronics, and computing to generate a simpler, more economical and reliable system. Because of the nature of my major, I tap into various aspects of engineering; From making a completely mechanical system to building a neural network and applying machine learning to automated systems. My passion, and the field I excel at and adapt to enormously would be software development.
While in college I had a co-op with the Engineering division at Amtrak where I was able to use python to analyze trends in train accidents at crossings over a 20 year period and the relationship between the frequency of these accidents and the equipment available at 200+ crossings across the country. I now work as a Software developer with Marcus by Goldman Sachs where I work with a variety of software and projects aimed towards providing the best service to our clients.
