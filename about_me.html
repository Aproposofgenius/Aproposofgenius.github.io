About Me

My name is Omomhene Chloe Eimunjeze, I am an international student from Abuja, Nigeria. I currently attend Vaughn College of Aeronautics and Technology in Queens, New York. where I study Mechatronics Engineering, a major which unites the principles of mechanics, electronics, and computing to generate a simpler, more economical and reliable system. Because of the nature of my major, I tap into various aspects of engineering to work on projects I think are amazing. From making a completely mechanical system to building a neural network and applying machine learning to automated systems. My passion, and the field I excel at and adapt to enormously would be software development. I generally started coding at 9, learning HTML, CSS and Python from various online sites, then moving onward to object oriented programming with C++, when I got to college I applied my knowledge of C++ to Robot-C, a robotics centered, C based language. After this, I picked up Data Structures and Algorithms, as well as complex computing, including O-notation. I directly apply these skills both in and out of the classroom. I am also adept in CAD design using Solidworks and Catia, Electronics using NI Multisim Finite Element Analysis using COMSOL Multiphysics and Matlab. Make sure to look through my project files for a more proof based understanding. Thank you
